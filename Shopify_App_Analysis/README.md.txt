# Shopify App Market Analysis (Power BI Dashboard)

## Overview
This project analyzes marketplace data to understand what drives the success of apps in the Shopify ecosystem. The Power BI dashboard visualizes developer performance, pricing strategies, customer reviews, and adoption trends.

You can download and open the full Power BI dashboard here:

## Key Findings
- The **strongest predictor of app success was review volume.** Apps with more customer reviews consistently ranked higher and attracted more installs.
- This suggests that **social proof and customer trust** play a significant role in app discovery and conversion.
- Pricing strategy alone did not determine success â€” many of the most successful apps used **mid-tier or usage-based pricing models** rather than being the lowest-cost option.

## Business Impact
Developers aiming to grow user adoption should invest in **review acquisition and customer engagement** strategies rather than competing solely on price.
